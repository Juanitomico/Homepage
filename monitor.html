Flat Screen Monitor

  • A modern, slim monitor.
    It has a flat surface and
    is lightweight compared to
    older monitors.

    • Lcd/Led Monitor

  • A type of flat-screen display
    that uses liquid crystals and
    a backlight to produce images.
    It's energy-efficient and commonly
    used in most modern devices.

    • Monochrome Monitor

  • A display that shows images
    in a single color on a background,
    typically black-and-white or green-and-black, used mostly in early computers.

    • CRT Monitor

  • An older, bulky type of monitor that
    uses electron beams and a phosphorescent
    screen to display images. It was
    commonly used before flat-screen
    technology became standard.
